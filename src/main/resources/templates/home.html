Devanagari Character Recognition

Devanagari character recognition system is built based on the Kohonen Neural Network approach. Line segmentation, word segmentation and character segmentation is done to identify character

Character recognition systems have become prevalent in recent years as the researches are progressing in the field of pattern recognition and also it helps in keeping records of handwritten scripts and data for future reference. For Nepali Computing community, this report aims to provide and assist in the making of Nepali handwritten and text recognition system. However, due to complex construct of Devanagari script, Matras and fused character could not be recognized.
